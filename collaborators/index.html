Collaborators

The AURA Protocol was conceived, authored, and maintained by Fausto David Cortés Rojas, M.Sc., currently a PhD candidate in engineering sciences. His academic contributions are registered under ORCID 0000-0001-5224-5710.

This work integrates independent research, metadata modeling, and interface development to promote transparency, attribution, and reproducibility in AI-assisted content generation.

The protocol structure, JSON schema, and documentation are designed and maintained as part of an academic initiative to establish a verifiable framework for acknowledging generative AI tools in scholarly and digital contexts.

This site is hosted at aura.org and serves as a reference for the continued evolution of the AURA standard.
